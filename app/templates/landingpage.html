Ways to help

Rotary Blood Group is public donation center with blood donation members in the changing health care system. We provide a blood and volunteer services across the country. With our national footprint, deep community roots and specialized services, we are the thread that connects people and resources together to fuel progress in transfusion medicine.

  • Inspiring people to give blood
  • Specialist blood donors and clinical supervision.
  • Producing a safe and ready blood supply
  • Increasing communication with our members.
  • High quality assessment, diagnosis and treatment.
  • Offering specialized patient services and pharmaceuticals
